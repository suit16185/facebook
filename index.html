Secure Your Facebook Profile & Change Your Password

Take control of your privacy by securing your Facebook account and changing your password to prevent unauthorized access.

Get Started

Why You Should Secure Your Facebook Account

Your Facebook account holds a lot of personal information. Securing it by changing your password regularly can prevent identity theft, unauthorized access, and privacy violations.

  • Protect your personal data from hackers.
  • Ensure privacy for your posts and personal details.
  • Monitor suspicious activities and unauthorized logins.

Steps to Secure and Change Your Password

1. Change Your Password

It's important to regularly change your Facebook password to something secure and unique. A strong password includes a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

Change Password Now

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Enhance your security by enabling two-factor authentication (2FA). This adds an additional layer of security, requiring a second code sent to your phone in addition to your password.

Enable 2FA

3. Review Active Sessions

Check the devices currently logged into your Facebook account. Log out from any sessions that you do not recognize or trust.

Review Sessions

4. Be Cautious of Phishing Scams

Be aware of emails or messages that ask for your personal information or login details. Facebook will never ask for your password via email or text message.

Learn About Phishing Scams
